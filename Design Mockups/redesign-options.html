Redesign options

Two sections, three directions each. Real palette, real fonts, real data.

Goal: kill the "form" feel — replace typing & tiny inputs with tapping, give choices visual weight, make each screen read like an artifact (your week / your plan) not a data-entry grid.

1 · Waste Log

Today: rows of chips under 4 headers; tapping one reveals a tiny number field to type into. The typing + small fields are what make it feel like a spreadsheet.

Bigger tap-cards instead of chips. Hours set with a − / + stepper, never typed. A running tray up top shows your week building in real time.
