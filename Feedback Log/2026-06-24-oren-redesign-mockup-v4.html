8 · The four waste typesadd-your-own + role-specific drains
✓ Users can add a type · role-specific drains explained

Why sort your waste into types?

Each type has a different escape hatch. Name the type, and you already know the kind of fix that works.

😴

Work that adds nothing

Meetings you weren't needed in, over-polishing, reports nobody reads.

→ Fix: cut it or shrink it.
⏳

Waiting & getting blocked

Stuck on approvals, handoffs, missing inputs or access.

→ Fix: unblock the bottleneck.
🤦

Redoing & fixing

Rework from unclear direction, errors, the same thing multiple times.

→ Fix: get it right the first time.
🔀

Switching & chasing

Tool-hopping, hunting for info, chasing people for status.

→ Fix: batch it, automate it, or ask once.
＋ Add your own type
Plus — drains specific to your role

The four types are the umbrella. When you pick a role, we add the drains unique to it (these already live in the catalog) so the list feels like your job, not a generic one.

Sales: lead research, asset hunting Engineering: PR & CI waiting Marketing: reports by hand, brand sign-off Founder/CEO: inbox overload, decisions routed through you
