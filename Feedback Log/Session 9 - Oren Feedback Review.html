"The week hrs should be per task. Of this time, how much could you actually cut? → This is too early to ask, and may change the numbers DOWN"

O3 Double Pareto — two-pass wizard METHODOLOGY Significant
"I strongly suggest this is upgraded to double Pareto, i.e., meeting that don't contribute. The 2nd round goes to elaboration, that cuts a really time consuming 1st try step"
What it does now Single-pass 5-step wizard: RoleStep → ContextStep → IntakeStep (select waste sources) → WeighStep (estimate hours for every source) → ResultsView. With 10-20+ sources selected, WeighStep is overwhelming.
What Oren wants Two-pass Pareto (a known "Pareto of Paretos" technique):
Pass 1: User picks broad waste categories + gives rough time estimates. Engine identifies the vital few categories.
Pass 2: User drills into ONLY those vital few categories, giving detailed estimates for specific sources within them.
This cuts down the overwhelming first-try step and produces more accurate results.
Impact if approved Major redesign — multi-session project. Requires: new category-level grouping step, intermediate results view, second-pass detail step, two-stage engine invocation, new state management. The idea has strong methodology merit (Oren literally invented this flow) but it's the single biggest change possible.
