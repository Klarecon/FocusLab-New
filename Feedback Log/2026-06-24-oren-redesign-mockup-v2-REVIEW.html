8 · The four waste types — now shows how it helpsnote 20

Why bother sorting your waste into types?

Because each type has a different escape hatch. Name the type, and you already know the kind of fix that works — no guessing.

😴

Doing things that don't need doing

Meetings you weren't needed in, over-polishing, reports nobody reads.

→ Fix: cut it or shrink it.
⏳

Waiting & getting blocked

Stuck on approvals, handoffs, missing inputs or access.

→ Fix: unblock the bottleneck.
🤦

Redoing & fixing

Rework from unclear direction, errors, the same thing twice.

→ Fix: get it right the first time.
🔀

Switching & chasing

Tool-hopping, hunting for info, chasing people for status.

→ Fix: batch it, automate it, or ask once.
